Select your visual content

Select either 'Video', 'Image' or 'Solid Colour' as a background, then upload the content if needed.

Select your audio content

Set your audio content; if the audio is contained within your video source select 'Video', if you want to upload a seperate audio file, select 'Audio'.

TIP: 'Advanced Audio' allows you to control the video audio seperately to the visuals. This is useful for only applying fades to the visuals but not the audio!

Add your lyrics

Insert your lyrics either by typing in the box or using copy and paste.

A table to insert timings will appear below.

By default each line will be shown seperately. Enabling 'Full verse mode' allows multiple lines to be shown on the screen at the same time. A blank line seperates each group of lines.

Select your text settings

Select how the text will look on screen. To see how your video and lyrics will look together at the current video time, click 'Preview' under the 'Preview Frame' heading.

Crop your visual content

Use this tool to crop your video or image as shown below, if you want to show the full image, ignore this step.

TIP: Hold shift to lock the aspect ratio to the same as the orriginal video, or hold control to lock the aspect ratio to 16:9.

Add the timings

Play your audio content and each time you want a new lyrics to appear click 'Start' button.

If the stop time is not specified it will be set to the start time of the following lyric. The video below shows how the cursor moves between the inputs as the 'Start' and 'Stop' buttons are clicked.

TIP: The line that is highlighted is the line(s) that will be shown at the current time of the audio.

TIP: You can press 'Q' on your keyboard instead of the 'Start' button and 'W' instead of the 'Stop' button.

Submit

Check all your settings and click 'Submit' to upload your media, then wait as we Add Your Lyrics!
